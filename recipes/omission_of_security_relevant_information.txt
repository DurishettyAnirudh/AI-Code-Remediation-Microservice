Short Description:
The product does not record or display information that would be important for identifying
the source or nature of an attack, or determining if an action is safe.

Secure Coding Checklist:
Do:
- Apply least privilege, validate input, and follow vendor hardening guides.
Don't:
- Non-Repudiation:Avoid situations leading toHide Activities:NOTE:The source of an attack will be difficult or impossible to determine. This can allow attacks to the system to continue without notice.

Language-Agnostic Guidance:
Document detection techniques and mitigation steps for this weakness.

Sample Fix Idea (Pseudo-code):
```pseudo
# High-level remediation stub
if receives_untrusted_input():
    validate_against_allowlist()
    enforce_least_privilege()
    log_and_monitor()
```
