name: Unprotected Primary Channel
languages: unspecified
tags: Base, Draft
---
Short Description:
The product uses a primary channel for administration or restricted functionality, but it
does not properly protect the channel.

Secure Coding Checklist:
Do:
- PHASE:Architecture and Design:Do not expose administrative functionnality on the user UI.
- PHASE:Architecture and Design:Protect the administrative/restricted functionality with a strong authentication mechanism.
Don't:
- Access Control:Avoid situations leading toGain Privileges or Assume Identity:Avoid situations leading toBypass Protection Mechanism

Language-Agnostic Guidance:
::PHASE:Architecture and Design:DESCRIPTION:Do not expose administrative functionnality on
the user UI.::PHASE:Architecture and Design:DESCRIPTION:Protect the
administrative/restricted functionality with a strong authentication mechanism.::

Sample Fix Idea (Pseudo-code):
```pseudo
# High-level remediation stub
if receives_untrusted_input():
    validate_against_allowlist()
    enforce_least_privilege()
    log_and_monitor()
```
