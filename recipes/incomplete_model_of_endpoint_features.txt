name: Incomplete Model of Endpoint Features
languages: unspecified
tags: Base, Incomplete
---
Short Description:
A product acts as an intermediary or monitor between two or more endpoints, but it does
not have a complete model of an endpoint's features, behaviors, or state, potentially
causing the product to perform incorrect actions based on this incomplete model.

Secure Coding Checklist:
Do:
- Apply least privilege, validate input, and follow vendor hardening guides.
Don't:
- Integrity:Other:Avoid situations leading toUnexpected State:Avoid situations leading toVaries by Context

Language-Agnostic Guidance:
Document detection techniques and mitigation steps for this weakness.

Sample Fix Idea (Pseudo-code):
```pseudo
# High-level remediation stub
if receives_untrusted_input():
    validate_against_allowlist()
    enforce_least_privilege()
    log_and_monitor()
```
